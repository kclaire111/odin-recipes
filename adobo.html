Chicken Adobo Recipe

Description

Chicken adobo is a staple dish in the traditional Filipino household.

It's made up of chicken thighs that have been marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, and other ingredients.

For a complete meal, serve the dish over a bed of rice.

ingredients

  • 750 g/1.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/3 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup + 2 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 small brown onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp peppercorns
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 2 green onions

Instructions

  1. Combine chicken thighs, garlic, soy sauce, white vinegar, and bay leaves in a bowl. Cover and marinate overnight.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a skillet over high heat. Remove chicken from marinade and place in the pan. Brown for at least a minute on each side.
  3. Remove chicken and set aside
  4. Add garlic and onions and cook for 1.5 minutes
  5. Add the leftover marinate, water, sugar and peppercorns. Bring to a simmer then turn down to medium heat. Simmer for 5 minutes
  6. Add chicken. Simmer uncovered for 20-25 minutes, turning chicken around 15 minutes until the sauce reduces to a thick syrup
  7. Remove chicken onto a place. Let the sauce simmer by itself for a bit. Then, return chicken to the skillet to coat in the glaze.
  8. Serve over rice
